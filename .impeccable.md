# Impeccable Design Context — Trailstead Guide

## Design Context

### Users
First-time family campers — parents with kids (ages 3–teens) who have never camped before. Planning happens at home, on a desktop or laptop, likely evenings or weekends. Anxious about logistics, overwhelmed by generic gear advice, want structure and confidence. Not adventurers — families who want one successful trip without embarrassing failures.

### Brand Personality
Calm, structured, trustworthy. Three words: **grounded, approachable, practical**. The emotional goal: replace overwhelm with confidence. Feel like a knowledgeable friend who has done this a hundred times, not a gear brand or a social media account.

### Aesthetic Direction
Anthropic.com-inspired: warm cream canvas (#F5F3EE), large editorial serif headings, open asymmetric grid, dark rounded feature cards floating on the cream background. Natural photography — soft light, families, real moments. No adrenaline. No neon. No startup-template blues. REI photography aesthetic crossed with Anthropic spatial generosity.

### Design Principles
1. **Openness over density** — Generous whitespace signals confidence and calm
2. **Editorial over template** — Asymmetric grids, type hierarchy from scale not decorations
3. **Imagery as evidence** — Photos show real camping moments, not aspirational lifestyle
4. **Structure is the product** — The numbered timeline, the gear list, the plan IS the value
5. **No adventure cosplay** — This is for families who want one good trip, not summit seekers
